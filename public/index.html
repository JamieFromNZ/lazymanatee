Cool language idea I had:
Alright so there are technically 600,000 words in the English language but only around 171,476 are actually used. If there are 20 consonants in the English alphabet and 5 vowels, that means you could have 25 one letter words, 5*20 + 20*5 = 100 two letter words, 20*5*20 + 5*20*5 = 2500 3 letter words so 25125 words in total that are all under 4 letters in length. Since the most frequent 1000 words in English account for 85% of words spoken, you could easily make all of these words super short. Now the main problem with this would be sacrificing patterns for efficiency but since we have lots of space left over, we don't need to worry much about that. edit: turns out you sound super dorky when you speak like this, "poci woki dak mu dako"
